Inducing Lucid Dreaming

Lucid dreaming can occur organically, but can also be induced with some effort. Below are some researched methods that will help you enter lucid dreams more frequently.

Wake Back To Bed

  • 1. Set an alarm for approximately 4-6 hours after you sleep. Proceed to fall asleep.
  • 2. After the alarm wakes you, write any dreams you remember down. Stay up for 30 to 60 minutes and complete a relaxing activity that does not include electronics which emit blue light.
  • 3. While you fall back to sleep replay the dream that was interrupted in your mind, searching for any inconsistencies that will help you realize you are dreaming. Inconsistencies can be anything like floating, missing fingers, locations you shouldn't be at.
  • 4. If you didn't have a dream, try repeating the mantra "next time I am dreaming, I will remember I'm dreaming."

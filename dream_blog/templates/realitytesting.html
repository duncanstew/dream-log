Reality Testing

Create a mental habit of performing sensory observations of the world around you. Eventually these habits will transition into your dreams and cause you to realize that you are dreaming!

How do these techniques work?

Every action below should produce normal and consistent results in your real life. In the dream state, your mind doesn't operate by the normal laws of physics and will create unusual circumstances.

Why does this work?

Dreams rarely hold up to scrutiny so performing reality checks will exploit the imperfections of the scene your brain generates. The laws of physics are rarely consistent in dreams. This will only work if you fully engage your mind in the reality check.

Breathing

One of the most effective techniques is pinching your nose and trying to breathe simultaneously. In a dream you will be able to breathe as if there is no obstruction.

Time

People experience this differently but for the most part time is dynamically generated. Glance at a clock or your phone's lock screen in a dream and the time will probably change randomly.

Text

Similar to the time check, if you glance at writing multiple times there is a high chance that each time the text will be different in a dream. In your own life, pay more attention to text in books, on the tv, on your phone, etc. If it doesn't change when you look back, you are probably awake!

Jumping

If you try jumping in a dream, you may end up floating or feel completely stuck in the ground. Either way, you will realize you are dreaming. Once again the laws of physics don't apply equally in dreams, leading ot unusual experiences.

Mirrors

Your brain won't be able to reflect your image in mirrors. Often mirrors in dreams will present an altered version of yourself. Take notice of the perfection of mirrors in your waking life so that when you cross when in a dream you will notice the imperfections.

Light Switches

Electrical devices will often fail to work in dreams. Try testing out light switches in your daily like as in the dream world, they probably won't affect anything.

Tattoos

Just like your hands are altered, people with tattoos report that their tattoos are often changed in dreams. Do a double take on your tattoo in a dream and see if it changes. In general, inspectingy your body closely will probably reveal some inconsistency.

Hands

Hands may be distorted in your dream. Try pushing one or two fingers through the palm of your other hands. In reality you will be met with resistance but in a dream your fingers might go right through your hands. Extra or missing fingers is very common too!
